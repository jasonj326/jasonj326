PIF12: The Circle

A 12-Year of Pay-it-Forward Journey Connecting the Brightest Stars to Light Up Lives.

The Ritual

I’ve been incredibly fortunate to meet so many kind, brilliant, and giving people. But most of you don't know each other.

What started as my personal ritual of making wishes and greetings is now evolving. PIF12 turns these ritual wishes into an annual blessing lucky charm—an Omamori (御守).

Minted as a non-transferable token (SBT), this blessing is recorded on the blockchain for good, forming our unbreakable bond.

My Core Commitments

The foundation of our 12-year journey.

The Annual Omamori

I am committed to sending out these digital blessing charms every single year. Let's allow this circle of goodwill to grow organically over the next 12 years.

The Physical Gathering

Taking this to the next level. I promise to host at least one onsite event per year, bridging the digital connection into a physical constellation of brilliant minds.

The Vision

Beyond my solid commitments, here are the directions we might explore together as a community. These are possibilities, driven by curiosity and collaboration.

dApp & Karma System

With the blessing of AI and the passion of potential contributors, we could build interactive dApp experiences and a support system to trace and foster "Karma" interactions within the circle.

Member-Driven Design

If feasible, I envision opening up the creative process. Future annual Omamori could be co-designed by members, making each token a true reflection of our shared journey.

Drawing the Constellation

Year 1

Years 2-11

Year 12

The Cycle Complete. The ultimate star map is revealed, and a fully connected tribe is established.
