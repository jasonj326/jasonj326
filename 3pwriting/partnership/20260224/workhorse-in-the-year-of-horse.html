Workhorse in the Year of Horse

2026-02-24 |
playbooks | playgrounds | partnership | y2026

In the Year of Horse, with inevitable incoming waves of AI, help me help you as a workhorse.

It is the best of times. Period.

In the past 55 days entering into 2026, I have created a demo website as part of my application for my dream position at Variant Fund (🤞), learned the structure of Git/GitHub by transitioning my website from Google Site (this article is me trying to put pieces together in Markdown), while starting my Japanese-learning journey and celebrating lunar new year with my family, and currently building the 10-year plan with a self-started 30-day AI x N sprint.

All of these are my efforts to solve one single problem: building a connected tribe.

I can't do this alone without AI, even though AI exacerbates my hunger for a tribe.

I can't build a tribe, or even a party-squad, without you, even though I probably don't know you yet.

Having fun while living as a full-force workhorse—this is my compass now.

Taking actions. Not fake actions that pretend we are working toward our goals: doing research, creating AI-toys, or randomly scrolling or posting how we feel.

Create and build something presented to the world.

Let it sink and compound.

I believe it is the best of times.

We can help each other grow and create a sustainable tribe with tech and abundant resources at arm's reach.

And more importantly, have fun down the road with our very limited time on earth.

Reach out. Help me help you.

Adapted from Day 8 of my 30-day AI x N Sprint.
